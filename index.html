ABOUT STROUDLEY

Stroudley Grill & PFC offers great cuisine for all ages. We are based in the East End of London and have built up a reputation for our authentic Grill food recipes, first class customer service and being a family friendly restaurant. Our food is always fresh, because we always ensure that all our food is cooked on the day, every day.

All our meats and chicken are halal.
